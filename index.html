METHODOLOGY

HOW WE CALCULATE

📊 BASELINE FIGURE

Our primary baseline is the Pentagon's own briefing to Congress (Mar 11, 2026): $11.3B for the first 6 days, plus $1B/day ongoing. This is the official, minimum figure from the Department of Defense itself.

🚀 MUNITIONS TRACKING

We track known interceptor costs (THAAD: $12.7M, PAC-3: $3.7M, Tomahawk: $2M) against reported usage from CSIS, Military Times, and DoD statements to build a bottom-up cost model.

✈ OPERATIONAL COSTS

Aircraft operating costs use DoD Comptroller FY2025 reimbursable flight-hour rates across 13 aircraft types deployed. Naval deployment costs use CBO naval operations cost models.

👤 PER-CITIZEN CALCULATION

U.S. per-citizen cost: Total cost ÷ 335M population. Israeli figure reflects U.S. military aid allocation. Iranian figure reflects estimated economic damage from strikes and export disruption.
